ABOUT

To put tokens into staking, holders need to fulfill two conditions: 1. To have Staker Protocol tokens in the wallet 2. To join someone's team Platform users can purchase Staker Protocol tokens from the Swap page or the Viper exchange.  By depositing tokens to staking contract, they are eligible to receive expected rewards that vary from 0,8-1,2% daily (approximately 1%) up to 365% on deposit, a maximum of 100 000 tokens per account.  Instead of rewarding holders by minting new tokens and putting them into circulation, Staker Protocol is based on deflation and has taxes on all kinds of transactions. All the collected taxes go to the staking pool from which staking rewards are coming. Starting taxes are 10% for most kinds of transactions. This tax can vary and go up, in case there are not enough tokens in the pool. Only in the case that taxes become too high, new tokens will be minted to reward the stakers, but chances for that are really low.   To be eligible to receive network rewards, users need to hold a Penny token in their wallets.

A deflationary staking platform that rewards holders from the tax on all transactions.
